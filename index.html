About Us

A Sanctuary for Your Nails

Sleek Nail Lounge is a luxury, non-toxic nail salon in Tse Addo, Accra, specialising in healthy nail care, nail restoration, and vegan nail extensions.

We use exclusively vegan, cruelty-free, ISO-certified products that are TPO-free and HEMA-free, providing safe, gentle, and long-lasting nail care. At Sleek Nail Lounge, healthy nails are the ultimate luxury.
