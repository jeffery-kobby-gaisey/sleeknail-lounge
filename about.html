About Us

A Sanctuary for Your Nails

Sleek Nail Lounge is a luxury, non-toxic nail salon in Tse Addo, Accra, specialising in healthy nail care, nail restoration, and vegan nail extensions. We offer vegan manicures, pedicures, gel polish, builder gel services, and nail extensions designed to strengthen, protect, and preserve natural nails.

We use exclusively vegan, cruelty-free, ISO-certified products that are TPO-free and HEMA-free, providing safe, gentle, and long-lasting nail care. Precision, hygiene, and respect for natural nail health define every service. Walk-ins are welcome, and appointments are available. At Sleek Nail Lounge, healthy nails are the ultimate luxury.
